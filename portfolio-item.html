Ahma Health Pre-assignment

Mobile app

Ahma Health is a private healthcare company serving private consumer and occupational healthcare customers. In recent years the company has started to lag behind its competitors when it comes to the company’s digital offering.

Ahma does have a responsive website and a native customer-facing app for consumer and occupational healthcare customers. Still, these have started to feel outdated and need to be developed visually and functionally.

Some use cases seem to occur frequently but are not supported well enough in the app. The assumption is that keeping these would create significant value for Ahma’s customers.
